Roast-Bot:

Roasting, Memes and More.


Made by Ole113#2421





Roast-Bot was made with two things in mind, to make people laugh; and to roast the living hell out of people. With this in mind Roast-Bot will unlikely have commands that a "multi-purpose" bot would have.

In "Info" there is info about Roast-Bot, upcoming updates, and past updates. The "command" tab gives updated information on Roast-Bot commands and simple command usage like what r!help gives. Finally the "Command Help" tab gives detailed information about Roast-Bot commands, much like what is returned by using r!<commamd> help.
